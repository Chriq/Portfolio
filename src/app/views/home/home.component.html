What is Scaredy Cat?

Great question! Scaredy Cat is an expression of my aesthetic taste, striking a delicate balance between the fun & whimsical, and the creepy & unsettling. My love of games was born in the nightmare fuel of Legend of Zelda games (particularly Ocarina of Time and Majora's Mask). It was nurtured in the existential dread of OMORI and OneShot. It matured in the philosphical pondering of Wanderstop and Clair Obscur: Expedition 33.

My mission is to tell compelling, introspective stories through video games and music. Together, we can explore the strange, wonderful, and sometimes horrifying parts of being human. Games have much to teach us about ourselves. All you have to do is play, listen, and don't be a scaredy cat!
